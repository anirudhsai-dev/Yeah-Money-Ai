# Yeah! Money - Smart Finance Made Simple

Yeah! Money is a professional personal finance management application designed to help you take control of your financial life. Built with React Native and Expo, it offers a seamless experience across mobile platforms.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 💰 Comprehensive Expense Tracking
*   **Quick Add**: Log your expenses, income, or transfers in seconds.
*   **Flexible Categories**: Organize your spending with a wide range of icons and categories.
*   **Multiple Accounts**: Manage Cash, Bank Accounts, Wallets, and more in one place.
*   **History**: Full searchable history of all your transactions with detailed notes.

### 🎯 Advanced Goal Management
*   **Financial Targets**: Set specific goals for things you're saving for.
*   **Progress Tracking**: Beautiful visual indicators showing how close you are to your targets.
*   **Inline Allocation**: Directly edit the allocated amount for any goal using a precision text input for granular control.
*   **Goal Reset**: Quickly reset any goal's allocated amount back to zero with a confirmation prompt.
*   **Smart Split Evenly**: Automatically distribute your total savings pool across all active goals or a specific selection of goals you choose.
*   **Emergency Fund Calculator**: A specialized tool that calculates your required safety net based on your actual monthly mandatory expenses selected from your history.

### 📊 Powerful Analytics
*   **Visual Spending Trends**: Compare your income vs. expenses over the last 6 months with interactive bar charts.
*   **Pie Chart Breakdown**: View a dynamic, high-quality pie chart of your expenses by category with total overlays.
*   **Expense Increase Alerts**: Intelligent detection system that warns you if you're spending more in specific categories than the previous month.
*   **Wealth Distribution**: See a visual breakdown of how your money is spread across your various accounts.

### 🎨 Premium UI/UX & Customization
*   **Lightning Splash**: A high-energy start-up animation featuring a thunder effect and spreading logo.
*   **Theme Engine**: Full support for **Light**, **Dark**, and **System Default** modes, easily switchable via the Settings menu.
*   **Interactive Guided Tour**: A beautiful step-by-step onboarding experience to help you master all features.
*   **Haptic Feedback**: High-quality tactile responses integrated throughout the app for a premium feel.
*   **Optimized Rendering**: Polished UI with fixed shadow artifacts and smooth transitions on both Android and iOS.

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 📋 Prerequisites
*   [Node.js](https://nodejs.org/) (v18+)
*   [Expo Go](https://expo.dev/go) app on your iOS or Android device.

### 🛠 Installation Steps

1.  **Clone the project**
    ```bash
    git clone <repository-url>
    cd YeahMoney_Updated
    ```

2.  **Install dependencies**
    ```bash
    npm install
    ```

3.  **Launch the development server**
    ```bash
    npx expo start
    ```

4.  **Open on your device**
    *   Scan the QR code displayed in your terminal using the **Expo Go** app (Android) or the **Camera app** (iOS).

---

## 📖 Usage Guide

1.  **Initialize Accounts**: Start by adding your current balances in the "Manage Accounts" section (accessible via the Home screen).
2.  **Log Transactions**: Use the large **+** button. Pick the right category to ensure your analytics are meaningful.
3.  **Configure Theme**: Tap the **Settings** gear on the Home screen to customize the app's appearance.
4.  **Setup Emergency Fund**: Go to the **Goals** tab and click the **Shield** icon. Select your bills/rent from your history to calculate your ideal safety net.
5.  **Distribute Savings**: Use **Split Evenly** in the Goals tab to instantly allocate your total savings across your priorities.
